Why City Square

A Community That Drives Business

City Square isn't just a shopping center — it's a neighborhood institution with loyal daily visitors and a diverse, established tenant mix.

Built-In Daily Foot Traffic
Our indoor marketplace draws hundreds of shoppers every morning, seven days a week — giving neighboring tenants consistent daily exposure.
300 Free Parking Stalls
Ample, free, at-grade parking is one of the biggest advantages for Kalihi — customers can visit multiple tenants in a single convenient trip.
100% Solar Powered
City Square's solar installation keeps operating costs low and demonstrates a commitment to sustainability that resonates with today's customers.
Established Neighborhood Anchor
A family shopping tradition for decades in the heart of Kalihi — City Square has deep community roots and a loyal, returning customer base.
